ABOUT US

Welcome to ReformRise, where we believe in the transformative power of education, skill-building, and justice advocacy. Our dedicated team is on a mission to make a meaningful difference in the lives of incarcerated individuals and those unfairly imprisoned. In a world striving for justice and inclusivity, where every individual deserves a second chance, that is where Reform rise come in an NGO dedicated to dismantling the walls of discrimination against the convicted and restoring their rightful place in society. Our mission is rooted in the belief that individuals who have served their sentences deserve the opportunity to rebuild their lives free from prejudice, exclusion, and stigma.
